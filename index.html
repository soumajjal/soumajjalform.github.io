Description

Capitalshop is an engaging fashion store website template for man’s, women’s or general online shops. In addition to that, even if you would like to create a website for an apparel brand, that’s what you can bring to fruition, too. Capitalshop is here for everyone, whether you are a beginner or a professional coder and developer. The template’s structure is very user-friendly, so you can quickly get the most out of it.

The bundle of features is vast, ensuring you rapidly speed up the eCommerce website creation. Slider, CTA buttons, top bar, social icons, testimonials, carousels and back-to-top button are just some of the amenities of Capitalshop. It also contains different internal pages, blog, Google Maps and a working contact form. Dive into the fashion industry with Capitalshop.
